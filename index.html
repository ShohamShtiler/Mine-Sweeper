How to Play Minesweeper

  • Objective: Clear the board without triggering any mines.
  • Starting the Game: Click on any cell to reveal it. The first click is always safe.
  • Understanding Cells:
    • Numbered Cells: Indicate how many mines are in the adjacent cells (including diagonals).
    • Empty Cells: No adjacent mines; clicking reveals surrounding cells automatically.
    • Mines: If clicked, you lose the game.
  • Marking Mines: Right-click (on mouse) to place a flag on suspected mine locations.
  • Winning the Game: Reveal all non-mine cells or correctly flag all the mines.
  • Lives: You have a limited number of lives. Stepping on a mine reduces your lives.
  • Timer: Keep track of your time; the goal is to clear the board as quickly as possible.
